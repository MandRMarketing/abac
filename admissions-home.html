Becoming a Stallion

ABAC Admissions

Choosing the right college is a major decision. Finding the right fit is important to your success, both academically and as a member of a community. At ABAC, we provide a unique experience: a thriving and engaged campus that encourages hands-on learning and values each student as an individual.

A Thriving Campus

With more than 50 student organizations, intercollegiate and recreational sports, and a calendar jam-packed with student events, Stallions remain engaged from admission to graduation.

Stallion Life

Academic Opportunity

Our name may say “Agriculture,” but our story is much bigger than that. With 26 degrees, minors, and certificates, ABAC offers small classes and individual attention to help students succeed.

Degrees & Programs

Affordable Education

We pride ourselves on affordability. Between scholarships, financial aid, and other affordability initiatives, we see more than half of all ABAC graduates leaving college with no student debt.
