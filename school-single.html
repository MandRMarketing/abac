ABAC Bainbridge: Foundational Studies in Southwest Georgia

Students who wish to start their educational journey at ABACs Bainbridge campus have several options from the School of Arts & Sciences:

Core Curriculum at ABAC Bainbridge

Students can complete the full General Studies pathway Associate of Science degree in the Core Curriculum at the Bainbridge site.

Bainbridge-Tifton 2+2 Plans

For Stallions pursuing a BS degree in Biology, History & Government, and Writing & Communication, ABACs 2+2 plans allow the first two years of study to be completed at ABAC Bainbridge, with the remaining two years completed at ABAC Tifton.

Jobs & Internships

Some of the most important learning takes place outside the classroom. Handshake provides Stallions with valuable opportunities to explore their field of study from the workplace through engaging and meaningful internships and other experiential learning programs. A large number of these internships end with full-time job offers; all of them end with an ABAC student who is excited about their future and prepared to face it head-on.

Handshake

Graduate & Professional School Opportunities

While ABACs faculty will prepare students for success, some careers require additional coursework beyond the baccalaureate level. Through the University System of Georgia and articulation agreements with other institutions, the School of Arts & Sciences offers a number of opportunities for seamless transfer, interview-free admission, or other ways for Stallions to easily progress to the next level of their education.

Faculty Spotlights

Engaged. Passionate. Knowledgeable. In the School of Arts & Sciences, our faculty take pride in their work and in the difference they make to every Stallion who calls ABAC home!

Dr. Sandra Giles

At ABAC, I work mainly with students in Writing and Communication, and Agricultural Communication, mentoringstudents in the areas of Creative Writing and Rhetoric. I also teach in the Rural Community Development program. I'm on the Board of Directors for the Southeastern Writers Association, and I'm also Vice-Chair of the Board for the leadership program of Locate South Georgia, an economic development consortium of 21 counties in South Georgia.
