Excellence In and Out of the Classroom

ABAC Academics

Education is at its best when classroom study meets real-world experience. At ABAC, we pride ourselves on offering an experiential, personalized approach to learning that gives graduates both the theoretical framework and the hands-on practice they need to thrive in their endeavors. Our focus on integrity both in and out of the classroom means that Stallions leave ABAC with a goal of making the world a better place.

A Trail for Every Stallion: ABAC Degrees & Programs

No matter where your interests lie, ABAC has a degree program to turn your passion into a rewarding career.

The Key to Our Academic Excellence: ABAC Faculty

Our nationally-recognized faculty are the core of our academic offerings. These dedicated members of the ABAC family bring to the table not only their unmatched experience and knowledge, but also a passion for teaching and helping students explore their interests and find the way towards a rewarding and fulfilling future.

Dr. Sandra Giles

At ABAC, I work mainly with students in Writing and Communication, and Agricultural Communication, mentoringstudents in the areas of Creative Writing and Rhetoric. I also teach in the Rural Community Development program. I'm on the Board of Directors for the Southeastern Writers Association, and I'm also Vice-Chair of the Board for the leadership program of Locate South Georgia, an economic development consortium of 21 counties in South Georgia.

Office of the Provost

The Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs (VPAA) is the Colleges Chief Academic Officer, and is ultimately responsible for all academic and student affairs matters. The Provost is responsible for:

  • Leading ABACs faculty
  • Planning and implementing academic and student policy
  • Providing guidance for the curriculum development
  • Promoting core values and academic integrity
  • Fostering academic excellence
